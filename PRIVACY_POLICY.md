# Privacy Policy — Video Study AI Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** May 14, 2026  
**Developer:** DailyAppsKit (https://dailyappskit.com)

## What This Extension Does

Video Study AI summarizes YouTube videos and generates study materials (flashcards, quizzes, Q&A) using Google's Gemini AI.

## Data We Collect

### Data stored locally on your device (never sent to us):
- Your Gemini API key (stored in Chrome sync storage)
- Language preference
- Daily usage count
- Premium subscription status

### Data sent to third-party services:
- **YouTube video URLs** are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
- No other data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal information (name, email, phone)
- Browsing history
- Video watch history
- Cookies or tracking data
- Analytics or telemetry

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses:
- **Google Gemini API** — to process video content and generate summaries. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Your API key is stored locally and sent directly to Google's API. We never see or store your API key.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| `storage` | Save your settings (API key, language) locally |
| `activeTab` | Read the current YouTube video URL when you click the extension |
| `host_permissions: youtube.com` | Inject the Study button on YouTube pages |

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally in your browser. Uninstalling the extension removes all data.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
